Senior Backend Engineer

[About the Role]
We are looking for a Senior Backend Engineer to join our Platform Engineering team. You will design, build, and scale the core services that power our AI-driven SaaS platform, serving millions of users worldwide. This is a high-impact role where you'll work on distributed systems, API design, and AI integration.

[Responsibilities]
1. Design and implement scalable, high-availability backend services and APIs
2. Architect and optimize distributed systems handling 100M+ daily requests
3. Lead technical design reviews and mentor junior engineers
4. Build and maintain CI/CD pipelines and infrastructure-as-code
5. Integrate LLM-powered features into production services (RAG, agents, embeddings)
6. Establish monitoring, alerting, and on-call practices for service reliability
7. Collaborate with product and ML teams to ship AI-native features

- Prior experience at a high-growth SaaS startup
