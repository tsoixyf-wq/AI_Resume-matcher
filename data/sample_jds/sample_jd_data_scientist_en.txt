Senior Data Scientist - ML/AI

Department: AI & Data Science
Location: New York, NY (Remote Friendly)
Salary Range: $150,000 - $200,000

[About the Role]
We are seeking a Senior Data Scientist to lead ML initiatives on our AI & Data Science team. You will develop and deploy machine learning models that directly impact our core product, from personalization engines to predictive analytics. Join us in building data-driven products that serve millions of users.

[Responsibilities]
1. Lead end-to-end ML projects from problem formulation through production deployment
2. Design, train, and evaluate machine learning models (classification, regression, recommendation systems)
3. Build and maintain ML pipelines using Python, Spark, and Airflow
4. Design and analyze A/B tests to measure the impact of ML-driven product changes
5. Collaborate with engineering teams to integrate models into production services
6. Develop dashboards and automated reports to monitor model performance and business metrics
7. Research and prototype novel ML approaches, including LLM-based solutions
8. Present findings and recommendations to technical and non-technical stakeholders

[Must-Have Requirements]
- Master's or PhD in Computer Science, Statistics, Mathematics, or related quantitative field
- 3+ years of industry experience in data science or machine learning engineering
- Expert-level Python (Pandas, NumPy, Scikit-learn, XGBoost, PyTorch/TensorFlow)
- Expert-level SQL with experience querying large-scale datasets (BigQuery, Snowflake, Redshift)
- Strong understanding of machine learning algorithms, statistical methods, and experiment design
- Experience deploying ML models to production with monitoring and iteration
- Proficiency with data visualization tools (Tableau, Looker, or Python libraries)
- Excellent communication skills with ability to explain complex analyses to diverse audiences

[Nice-to-Have]
- Experience with deep learning frameworks (PyTorch, Transformers, HuggingFace)
- Knowledge of NLP, LLMs, RAG, or recommendation systems
- Experience with big data processing (Spark, Hive, Databricks)
- Familiarity with ML infrastructure (MLflow, Kubeflow, Weights & Biases)
- Experience with cloud ML services (AWS SageMaker, GCP Vertex AI)
- Published research papers or conference presentations
- Experience in e-commerce, fintech, or ad-tech domains
